Hello, I am an artist from Dallas, PA. Thanks for visiting.

Drawing has been my hobby since I was a small child.

My style is based on cartoons I grew up watching, like 'FlapJack', 'SuperJail', 'Ren N Stimpy', and things like that. Although I draw the things that are inside my own imagination.

I like to draw with pencils or markers, and I have a lot of fun with Adobe Illustrator.
